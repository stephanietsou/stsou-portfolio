hi, again!

UX designer and Psychology graduate passionate about designing with empathy and awareness of others. An inquisitive individual and always looking to figure out the “why” in everything.

Based in San Jose, CA, I am currently pursuing a certificate in UX/UI Design from UC Berkeley. I hold a BA in Psychology and Minor in Education from UCB as well.

I've worked in several different client- and customer-facing roles –from being a behavioral technician working with kids on the autism spectrum, to working in sales for a small insurtech start-up, I've developed a profound understanding of the customer journey and have become accustomed to working in fast-paced environments that require me to think quick on my feet.

Outside of design, I enjoy spending time with family and friends, scouting out new coffee shops to try, and making my sister take candid photos of me around the city.

If you want to learn more, shoot me a message! Or, take a peek at my resume here. Let's be friends!
